It’s been more than a hundred years, and consumer affection for RC Cola refuses to fizzle out. As one of the original American colas, RC has grown to be the brand choice of millions across the world. RC MIGHTY RAIN

RC Q flavors were first introduced under the brand name Nehi in 1920. Founder Claud A. Hatcher overheard a salesman describe a competitor’s glass bottle as being “knee-high,” and he used the name to introduce what would become America’s best-known line of flavored, carbonated soft drinks.
